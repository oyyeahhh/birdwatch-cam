This directory mixes two sources. Filenames are kebab-case scientific names.

## 1. AvianVisitors set (20 files, CC BY-NC-SA 4.0)

These PNG cutouts come from
[AvianVisitors](https://github.com/Twarner491/AvianVisitors) by Teddy Warner
(built on [BirdNET-Pi](https://github.com/mcguirepr89/BirdNET-Pi)), used under
**CC BY-NC-SA 4.0**: https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc-sa/4.0/
Changes: resized to 640 px max dimension.

- Attribution — keep this notice with the images.
- NonCommercial — fine for CIJE's school/classroom use; do not sell.
- ShareAlike — adaptations of these files stay under the same license.

Files: agelaius-phoeniceus, baeolophus-bicolor, cardinalis-cardinalis,
cyanocitta-cristata, dryobates-pubescens, haemorhous-mexicanus,
junco-hyemalis, melanerpes-carolinus, melospiza-melodia, mimus-polyglottos,
passer-domesticus, poecile-atricapillus, quiscalus-quiscula,
sitta-carolinensis, spinus-tristis, sturnus-vulgaris,
thryothorus-ludovicianus, troglodytes-aedon, turdus-migratorius,
zenaida-macroura.

## 2. Vintage natural-history plates (2 files, public domain)

Two species don't exist in the AvianVisitors set. Their cutouts were
produced from public-domain / no-known-restrictions scans on Wikimedia
Commons (background removed, cropped, resized to 640 px max). The underlying
artworks are out of copyright; our modifications are released CC0.

| File | Source artwork |
|---|---|
| poecile-carolinensis | *Bird-Lore* (1916) chickadee plate, Louis Agassiz Fuertes, via BHL/Commons (no known restrictions) |
| zonotrichia-albicollis | L. Prang & Co. chromolithograph, *Singing Birds* sparrow plate (1874), via Commons (public domain) |

These two can be regenerated in the AvianVisitors kachō-e style with
upstream's `frame/generate_illustrations.py` (Gemini API, billing required)
whenever tighter visual consistency is wanted.
